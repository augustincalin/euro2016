How can you gain and became famous?

Easy: If you guess the result (1, X or 2) you get 3. If you guess the number of goals a team scores you receive 1.

Example

Let's suppose Denmark is playing against Greece and you estimate Denmark - Greece will be 2 : 1.

If the real score is...

Denmark - Greece 2 : 1 - In this case you will get 3 for identifying the winner + 1 for guessing the number of goals Denmark scores + 1 for guessing the number of goals Greece scores. That's 5 in total.

Denmark - Greece 2 : 0 - You will receive 3 for correctly identifying the winner + 1 point for guessing the number of goals Denmark scores. Total: 4.

Denmark - Greece 1 : 1 - You will receive only 1 for guessing the number of goals Greece scores.

Denmark - Greece 5 : 2 - In this case you will receive 3 for guessing the winner.

Denmark - Greece 1 : 2 - In this case you will receive 0,-. You guessed nothing, you receive nothing!

The workflow

You can bet on any further match. You can change your bet any time for a further match. Obviously you can't change a bet for a match that already started. Alexander, don't even try it! Once the match started, that's it, you can't bet on it.

The numbers are updated after each game except when I'm on support rota. In such case a slight delay may occur.

Have fun!
